Our Vision

Link Al Amel Contracting Company

Link Al Amel Construction Initiated to contribute to and be a part of Saudi Arabia's rapidly increasing industrial activity in the fields of heavy equipment rentals, manpower supply, and material supply, among other things. We are a major source of heavy equipment rental that lifts, transports, digs, powers, and creates. We make certain that the equipment we give is dependable, ready to use, and efficient. Our services are available to heavy process industries such as oil refineries, construction firms, petrochemical plants, mining companies, and so on. We provide highly qualified experts and skilled laborers to deliver the best top, middle, and lower level workforce in the short, medium, and long term.
